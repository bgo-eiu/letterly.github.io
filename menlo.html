Menlo Forward

Leveraging a loophole in California’s housing laws, a mammoth project has been proposed for the former Sunset Magazine site on Willow Road in Menlo Park. The project includes 37-, 33-, and 18-story skyscrapers - with the largest to become the tallest building between San Francisco and Los Angeles. This site is in a quiet, residential neighborhood. We are opposed to this proposal and put forth the following:

  • We support the City of Menlo Park’s state-approved housing development plan that supports our diverse and growing community through the significant increase in availability and affordability of housing.
  • We support responsible, mixed-use development at 80 Willow Road that aligns with Menlo Park’s approved housing development plan and provides for historic preservation.
  • We urge the City to fight this outrageous proposal which overrides local regulations - and common sense. Providing far more business than residential space, the project is wildly out of scale for Menlo Park and will worsen the City’s housing deficit and traffic congestion. It will permanently alter the City’s quality of life and character, creating a nightmare for Menlo Park and the cities around us.

Join us! Together we can affect the outcome of this project. We call on the residents of Menlo Park and neighboring communities to oppose this egregious proposal and work toward a solution that benefits all.
